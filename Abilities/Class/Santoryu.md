# Santoryu

Santoryu, also known as Three Sword Style, is a mythical class that will be based on abilities that Roronoa Zoro uses.

### Passives

All Slash Type Weapons deal an extra 32 damage per hit.

Uru Tora Gari is the first automatically unlocked ability for Santoryu. The ability allows for the player to launch forward and swing the blades dealing damage. If the player is in the air while using this ability, they will launch to the target they are aiming at.
