# Enies Lobby Guide

Once the Water 7 Main Questline is complete, speak to Sanji (W7) or the Teleporter to warp to Enies Lobby and begin questing!

### Main Quest

To start the Main Quest, speak to Sanji (EL) near the island teleport location.
