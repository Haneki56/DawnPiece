# Morioh Island Guide

This island is accessible from the Donation Island Menu in the Teleporter after purchasing the Tenryuibto Rank from the [Donation Store](https://adventures-at-sea.tebex.io/).

### A Quest

To start the A Quest, speak to Jotaro Kujo (JJ4) near the island teleport location.

| Quest Code| Quest Name                | Quest Objective|
|-----------|-----------                |-----------|
| A01       | Welcome To Morioh!        |Find Josuke’s House somewhere in town.|
| A02       | An Intruder!              |Defeat Anjuro Katagiri’s stand Aqua Necklace in Jotaro’s House.|
| A03       | Morioh’s Protector!       |Talk to Josuke Higashikata (JJ4) in his garage to complete the quest.|
| A04       | Bad Company!              |Defeat Keicho Nijimura’s stand Bad Company in the Nijimura Residence southeast of Josuke’s House.|
| A05       | The Hand!                 |Defeat Okuyasu Nijimura’s stand The Hand in their house.|
| A06       | Keicho’s Death!           |Talk to Okuyasu Nijimura (JJ4) in front of his house to complete the quest.|
| A07       | The Lock!                 |Defeat Tamami Kobayashi at the intersection by the Nijimura Residence.|
| A08       | An Impersonator!          |Talk to Koichi Hirose (JJ4) at the intersection by the Nijimura Residence to complete the quest.|
| A09       | Surface!                  |Defeat Toshikazu Hazamada’s stand Surface down the street from the Nijimura Residence.|
| A10       | Koichi’s Admirer!         |Talk to Yukako Yamagishi (JJ4) at the intersection to complete the quest.|
| A11       | Love Deluxe!              |Defeat Yukako Yamagishi in front of Owson.|
| A12       | Italian Food!             |Talk to Okuyasu (JJ4) in front of the Trattoria Trussardi at the Town Square to complete the quest.|
| A13       | Pearl Jam!                |Defeat Tonio Trussardi’s stand Pearl Jam in the Trattoria Trussardi.|
| A14       | A Chef’s Pride!           |Talk to Josuke (JJ4) in front of the Trattoria Trussardi at the Town Square to complete the quest.|
| A15       | Red Hot Chili Pepper!     |Defeat Akira Otoishi’s stand Red Hot Chili Pepper in the park in the Town Square.|
| A16       | Josuke’s Old Man!         |Talk to Joseph Joestar (JJ4) in the train station to complete the quest.|
| A17       | The Mangaka!              |Talk to Toshikazu Hazamada (JJ4) in front of Rohan’s House to complete the quest.|
| A18       | Heaven’s Door!            |Defeat Rohan Kishibe’s stand Heaven’s Door in his backyard.|
| A19       | Writer’s Block!           |Talk to Rohan Kishibe (JJ4) in front of Rohan’s House to complete the quest.|
| A20       | The Ghost Alley!          |Find the Ghost Alley somewhere in town.|
| A21       | The Ghost Girl!           |Talk to Reimi Sugimoto (JJ4) in the Ghost Alley to complete the quest.|
| A22       | A Stolen Bag!             |Talk to Kosaku Kawajiri (JJ4) at the end of the Ghost Alley to complete the quest.|
| A23       | Harvest!                  |Defeat Shigekiyo Yangu’s stand Harvest at the intersection near the Ghost Alley.|
| A24       | Stolen Face!              |Talk to Yukako (JJ4) in front of Beauty Salon Cinderella at the Town Square to complete the quest.|
| A25       | Cinderella!               |Defeat Aya Tsuji’s stand Cinderella in the Beauty Salon Cinderella.|
| A26       | The Killer!               |Talk to Jotaro (JJ4) in front of Beauty Salon Cinderella at the Town Square to complete the quest.|
| A27       | Kira’s House!             |Find Kira's House somewhere in town.|
| A28       | Atom Heart Father!        |Defeat Yoshihiro Kira in Kira’s House.|
| A29       | Rock Paper Scissors!      |Talk to Rohan (JJ4) in front of Kira’s House to complete the quest.|
| A30       | Boy II Man!               |Defeat Ken Oyanagi’s stand Boy II Man in front of Kira’s House.|
| A31       | The Alien?!               |Talk to Mikitaka Hazekura (JJ4) at the end of the road by the tower to complete the quest.|
| A32       | Earth Wind And Fire!      |Defeat Mikitaka Hazekura by the tower.|
| A33       | A Flood Of Stand Users!   |Talk to Koichi (JJ4) at the end of the road by the tower to complete the quest.|
| A34       | Highway Star!             |Defeat Yuya Fungami’s stand Highway Star by the tunnel on the south end of town.|
| A35       | Enigma!                   |Defeat Terunosuke Miyamoto’s stand Enigma at the intersection by Rohan’s House on the north end of town.|
| A36       | Cheap Trick!              |Defeat Masazo Kinoto’s stand Cheap Trick on the street at the west end of town.|
| A37       | Only Kira Remains!        |Talk to Joseph (JJ4) in front of the park west of the town square to complete the quest.|
| A38       | Killer Queen!             |Defeat Yoshikage Kira’s stand Killer Queen in the park west of the town square to complete the quest.|
| A39       | Rest In Peace!            |Talk to Reimi (JJ4) in the Ghost Alley to complete the questline.|

Item Rewards: 16,850 Belly<br>
