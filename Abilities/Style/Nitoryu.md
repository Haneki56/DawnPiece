# Nitoryu Style

Nitoryu, also known as Two Sword Style, is a Legendary Class in the Slash Line that is based on abilities that Roronoa Zoro and Kozuki Oden use.

### Passives

All Slash Type Weapons deal an extra 16 damage per hit.
