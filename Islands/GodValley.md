# God Valley Guide

This island is accessible from the Side Island Menu in the Teleporter after completing Egghead.

### A Quest

To start the A Quest, speak to Rocks (GV) near the island teleport location.

| Quest Code| Quest Name                | Quest Objective|
|-----------|-----------                |-----------|
| A01       | God’s Knights!            |Defeat Shepherd Sommers, Manmayer Gunko and Satchels Maffey along the road to the town.|
| A02       | A Distorted World!        |Talk to Dragon (GV) in town to complete the quest.|
| A03       | Save My Children!         |Talk to Red Haired Mother (GV) in the house by Dragon to complete the quest.|
| A04       | A Figarland Affair!       |Defeat Figarland Garling in the town.|
| A05       | The King’s Possession!    |Defeat Imu (Saturn) in the town.|
| A06       | The Hero Of The Marines!  |Defeat Monkey D. Garp on the north side of town to complete the questline.|

Item Rewards: 25,000 Belly, Epic Shard<br>
Bounty Reward: 25,000,000

### B Quest

To start the B Quest, speak to Roger (GV) near the island teleport location.

| Quest Code| Quest Name                | Quest Objective|
|-----------|-----------                |-----------|
| B01       | The Rocks Pirates!        |Defeat Gil Bastar, Barbell and Ganzui along the west coast of the island.|
| B02       | Notorious Criminals!      |Defeat Ochoku, Captain John and Kyo along the west coast of the island.|
| B03       | Future Yonko!             |Defeat Young Kaido, Newgate and Linlin along the west coast of the island.|
| B04       | Scourge Of The Sea!       |Defeat Shiki, Don Marlon and Xebec on the north side of the island to complete the questline.|

Item Rewards: 44,500 Belly, Legendary Shard<br>
Bounty Reward: 44,500,000

| Quest Code| Quest Name                | Quest Objective|
|-----------|-----------                |-----------|
| C01       | The Roger Pirates!        |Defeat Rowing, Millet Pine and Donquino along the east coast of the island.|
| C02       | A Crew Of Monsters!       |Defeat Crocus, Moon Issac Jr. and Sunbell along the east coast of the island.|
| C03       | The Strongest Crew!       |Defeat Scopper Gaban, Silvers Rayleigh and Gol D. Roger along the east coast of the island to complete the questline.|
