# Egghead Island Guide

Once the Onigashima Main Questline is complete, speak to Speak to Momonosuke (ONI) or the Teleporter to warp to Egghead and begin questing!

### Main Quest

To start the Main Quest, speak to Jinbe (EGG) near the island teleport location.
